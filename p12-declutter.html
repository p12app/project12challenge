How to Use This App
What is this?
The Project 12 Declutter Challenge. Your goal is to remove 465 items from your home in 30 days. Trash them, donate them, or sell them — they just have to leave.

How to log items
Enter the number of items you removed and tap Add to My Total. Notes are optional. Your running count updates automatically.

What counts?
Anything that leaves your home. Clothes, books, kitchen items, decor, paperwork, toiletries, old electronics — if it's gone, it counts.

What doesn't count?
Everyday trash you would have thrown out anyway. The goal is to clear out things that have been sitting and taking up space.

View your entries
Tap any entry in your log to see the full details -- what you removed, how many items, and your running total up to that point.

Save to your home screen
iPhone: Open this link in Safari. Tap the Share button at the bottom. Tap Add to Home Screen. Toggle Open as Web App to on. Tap Add.

Android: Open this link in Chrome. Tap the three dots in the top right and tap Add to Home Screen. Tap Add.
