🏔 Avalanche — Any extra money goes to the highest interest rate debt first. Once paid off, that payment rolls to the next. Saves the most money overall.

⛄ Snowball — Any extra money goes to the smallest balance first. Once paid off, that payment rolls to the next. Builds momentum with faster wins.

Which is better? Avalanche saves more money. Snowball builds motivation. Pick the one you'll stick with.
